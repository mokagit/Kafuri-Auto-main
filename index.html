Our services

We Provide Great Services For your Vehicle

  • Engine Repair

    We fix parts or systems within an engine, such as a worn-out timing belt, a faulty ignition coil, or a damaged head gasket.

  • Brake Repair

    We fix or replace the mechanical parts of a vehicle's braking system, such as brake pad or rotor replacement.

  • Tire Repair

    We do replace, balance tires and also fix punctured tire, typically by plugging or patching it.

  • Battery Repair

    We do repalcement, recharging and restoring a battery to its original working condition.

  • Steering Repair

    We fix issues with a vehicle's steering system to improve handling and safety.

  • Suspension Repair

    We do Inspection, replacement and maintenance of a vehicle's suspension system, which is the connection between the wheels and the car.

  • Exhaust Repair

    We repair or replace parts of your vehicle's exhaust system that have become damaged, worn out, or broken. This system includes a variety of components, including the exhaust manifold, catalytic converter, muffler, and exhaust pipes.

  • Transmission Repair

    We Fix specific transmission components, Adjusting the throttle cable, Changing or flushing the transmission fluid, Replacing seals or gaskets, and Repairing the clutch.
